About Me





Hi, my name is Ryan and this is a little bit about me... I am originally from Florida but currently reside in Georgia. As of May 2017, I am a graduate from the Georgia Institute of Technology. After what seemed to be a very long 5 years, I finally completed my BS in Industrial Engineering. During my time here at Georgia Tech I was lucky enough to be the punter for the Yellow Jacket's football team, which I am mostly remembered for my fake punt against Miami... Although I was BODY SLAMMED on national television, I still managed to get the first down!


I have always had a passion for Technology and love how it has the ability to improve the lives of the people who utilize it! I started this journey to becoming a Full Stack Web Developer not too long ago by joining Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp. I have learned a tremendous amount in such a short time and love the challenges that come along with development. I am excited and looking forward to the opportunities that lie ahead!
